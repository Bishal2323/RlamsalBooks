# Enable Email/Password Login
To make the login portal work, you need to turn on the "Switch" in Firebase.

1. Go to your **[Firebase Console](https://console.firebase.google.com/)**.
2. Click your project (`r-lamsal-books-backend`).
3. In the left menu, click **Build** -> **Authentication**.
4. Click **Get Started** if asked.
5. Click the **Sign-in method** tab.
6. Click **Email/Password**.
7. Keep "Email/Password" **Enabled**.
8. Click **Save**.

Now you can go to your website, click **Login**, create an account, and sign in!
